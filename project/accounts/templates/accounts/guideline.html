MY CAMPUS Content Policy
MY CAMPUS is a platform for communities to discuss, connect, and share in an open environment. The nature of this content might be funny, serious, offensive, or anywhere in between. While participating, it’s important to keep in mind this value above all others: show enough respect to others so that we all may continue to enjoy MY CAMPUS for what it is.
While MY CAMPUS generally provides a lot of leeway in what content is acceptable, here are some guidelines for content that is not. Please keep in mind the spirit in which these were written.
1. Prohibited content
Is spam
Is illegal
Is involuntary pornography
Is sexual or suggestive content involving minors
Encourages or incites violence
Threatens, harasses, or bullies or encourages others to do so
Is personal and confidential information
Impersonates someone in a misleading or deceptive manner

In addition to not submitting prohibited content, the following behaviors are prohibited on MY CAMPUS
2. Prohibited behavior
Asking for votes or engaging in vote manipulation
Breaking MY CAMPUS or doing anything that interferes with normal use of MY CAMPUS

We have a variety of ways of enforcing our rules, including, but not limited to
3. Enforcement
Asking you nicely to knock it off
Asking you less nicely
Temporary or permanent suspension of accounts
Adding restrictions to, accounts
Adding restrictions to MY CAMPUS boards
Removal of content
Banning of MY CAMPUS boards

We prevent abusive behavior and block abusive content that is reported by other users.
So please use our ‘report abuse’ function!
If your account is banned, you could not use MY CAMPUS any more, FOREVER!
